BookHub

Reading is a group activity

Books bring joy, and joy should be shared

We created BookHub because we love stories. Since the beginning of civilization, stories were created to be shared... we think it's kind of the whole point! Book clubs have been around longer than any of us can remember, and they have stayed mostly the same. We though it was time for a new approach. BookHub is that new approach, and here's how:

Connecting People

This is a place holder for an explanation on how BookHub connects likeminded individuals to share their love for books and functions as a quasi-social media platform for readers.

Encouraging Future Authors

This a place holder for how BookHub can connect readers with new or unpublished authors, and authors can connect with their community and build a name for themselves.

Reading for Everyone

This is a place holder for how BookHub can provide a resource for free reading materials, access to audiobooks, partnerships with local and online libraries, and nonprofits to increase access both online and in schools.
